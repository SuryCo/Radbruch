'Moin' … sagt man bei uns

Radbruch is a quiet idyllic village in the flat lands of Neidersachsen. The people are simple and friendly. Life moves at a different pace here. Everyone takes care of thier neighbours and at 6 in the evening, everything is closed. Radbruch has 2200 residents. It is well connected to Hamburg and Lüneburg with Metronom with trains every hour. There are a lot of horses here and a bunch of tractors too.
